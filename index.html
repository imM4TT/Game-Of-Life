Game Of Life

This is your game of life.

Every black square represent a living cell, white represents a dead cell

You can click on cell to born/kill it, therefore it's the awakening of the life algorithm.

Only 2 Rules :

*If a dead cell has 3 neighbors, it's born.
*If a living cell has 2 or 3 neighbors it stays alive, otherwise it dies.
